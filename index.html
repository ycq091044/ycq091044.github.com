Biography

I am currently a senior undergrad at School of Cyber Security in Shanghai Jiao Tong University. I work as a Student Researcher in Advanced Network Laboratory (ANL) at the Department of Computer Science, under the supervision of Associate Professor Dr. Gao Xiaofeng.

This Summer, I fortunately worked as a Research Intern with GCN in Community QA, in University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign (UIUC). My advisor is Dr. Hari Sundaram.

Recently, I join Tencent Social Ads in Beijing Division to do research, on Reinforcement Learning in Advertisement Price Bidding, until next year before my graduation.

* This year, I will apply for 2019 Fall CS Ph.D. Programs on Data Mining and Machine Learning, especailly those interdisciplinary, such as Social Networks, Advertising and Multimedia.
