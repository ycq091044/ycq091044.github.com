I am a master PhD student in CS @ UIUC. My advisor is Prof. Jimeng Sun. I finished B.S. in Shanghai Jiao Tong University (SJTU) from 2015 to 2019.

I am building self-supervised learning and tensor methods on healthcare problems. My previous works include graph neural network, social polarization mining, RTB, event popularity prediction, etc. Previously, I worked with Prof. Tarek Abdelzaher, Prof. Xiaofeng Gao and Prof. Gongshen Liu.
