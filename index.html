I am a master PhD student in CS @ UIUC. I finished my B.E. in Shanghai Jiao Tong University (SJTU) from 2015 to 2019. I started my research with Prof. Xiaofeng Gao.

Now I am fortunate to work with Prof. Jimeng Sun in SunLab. I am recently working on self-supervised contrastive learning and tensor methods with EEG sleep data. My previous works include GCN, drug recommendation, social polarization mining, RTB, event popularity prediction, etc. In UIUC, I also worked with Prof. Tarek Abdelzaher.
