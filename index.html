I am an M.S. student in the Department of Computer Science, University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign. Before that, I received my B.E. in Information Security, Shanghai Jiao Tong University, and won the title Outstanding Graduates of Shanghai. I have also taken an intern in Tencent Social Ads for six months.

Currently, my advisor is Professor Tarek F. Abdelzaher, We plan to model, analyze, and simulate social media information propagation and how it both affects and is affected by people's beliefs.
